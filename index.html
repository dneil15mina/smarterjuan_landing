Opensource Softwares and Services we Provide

Moodle LMS

Open-source Learning Management System

Moodle LMS is a flexible, open-source learning platform designed to provide educators with a powerful set of tools for creating dynamic, engaging online learning experiences. It offers customizable features, seamless integration, and a user-friendly interface, making it ideal for schools, universities, and organizations of all sizes to effectively deliver education and training.

Mattermost

Mattermost is a secure collaboration platform for accelerating mission critical work in complex environments

Mattermost is a secure, open-source collaboration platform designed to enhance communication and teamwork in complex environments. It provides real-time messaging, file sharing, and integration with various tools, enabling teams to streamline mission-critical work while maintaining full control over data and privacy. Ideal for organizations that require secure, efficient, and customizable solutions for their collaboration needs.

Plane

Open-source Project Management Software

Plane is an open-source project management software that helps teams organize, track, and manage their tasks efficiently. With intuitive features for planning, collaboration, and progress tracking, Plane allows teams to streamline workflows, stay organized, and enhance productivity, all while offering the flexibility and control of an open-source platform. Ideal for businesses looking for a customizable and cost-effective project management solution.

Metabase

Open-source Data Visualization and Exploration Tool

Metabase is an open-source data exploration and visualization tool that enables users to effortlessly connect to their data sources, build interactive dashboards, and uncover valuable insights. Its intuitive design makes it accessible for users of all technical skill levels, providing a powerful yet user-friendly solution for turning data into actionable information.
